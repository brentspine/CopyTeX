# CopyTeX Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

CopyTeX is a browser extension that lets you copy LaTeX source from KaTeX and MathJax formulas on any webpage.

## What is stored

CopyTeX saves the following data locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`:

- **Settings** - your KaTeX/MathJax toggles, list mode (blacklist or whitelist), and your domain list.
- **Usage statistics** - a count of formulas copied, character counts, and the hostnames where copies occurred. This data stays on your device.

## What is not collected

CopyTeX does not transmit any data to external servers. No analytics, no ads, no third-party services.

## Permissions

- **Access to all websites** - needed so the extension can find math formulas on any page. All processing happens locally.
- **Active tab** - used only to pre-fill the current site hostname when you open the domain list editor.
- **Storage** - used to save your settings and local statistics.

## Deleting your data

Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data. You can also clear it from your browser's extension storage settings.
